Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

TrialGuard ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service at mytrialguard.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use TrialGuard:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily by Premium users to receive trial reminders
  • Trial data — the services and dates you enter to track your free trials
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we never store your card details
  • Usage data — basic analytics about how you use the app (page views, clicks)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Send you email reminders about upcoming trial expirations (Premium users only)
  • Process your subscription payments via Stripe
  • Improve our service and fix bugs
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage

Your trial data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not store your trial data on our servers. Your email address is stored securely and used only to send reminders.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Stripe — for payment processing. View their privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy
  • Resend — for sending reminder emails. View their privacy policy at resend.com/privacy
  • Vercel — for hosting our app. View their privacy policy at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the service providers listed above, and only as necessary to operate TrialGuard.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from reminder emails at any time
  • Cancel your Premium subscription at any time

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to operate the service. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

TrialGuard is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
